Artist's Note

All artworks displayed here are thoughtfully designed and handcrafted by me. Each piece is made with care, attention to detail, and an intent to bring character into a space.

Some artworks may be available for purchase, while others may be sold out. Custom pieces can be created on request to suit your décor or personal preferences.

As every artwork is handmade, recreations may vary slightly, often improving through the creative process.

For custom requests, availability, or any queries, feel free to get in touch.

The Artist

For years, my world was defined by the fast-paced environment of merchandise management. I had the privilege of working with multiple companies, but deep down, I always knew that my hands were meant to create, not just manage.

Today, I have traded spreadsheets for sketchbooks. I consider myself a perpetual student and a self-taught explorer, currently diving deep into the intricate worlds of Mandala, Madhubani, Gond, and Warli painting, as well as the tactile beauty of Texture and Lippan art.

Painting and drawing aren't just what I do; they are what I live for. Every piece you see here is a result of that joy—a handmade exploration of tradition, texture, and the simple beauty of a line. Welcome to my world of "Shiv Kriti," where every stroke tells a story of rediscovery.
